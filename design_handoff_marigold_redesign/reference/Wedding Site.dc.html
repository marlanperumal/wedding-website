Getting there
FROM CAPE TOWN CBD
  1. Get onto the N1 heading north, towards Paarl.
  2. Take Exit 23 (Okavango Road / R302) towards Durbanville.
  3. Follow Okavango Road north into Durbanville, then continue onto Klipheuwel Road heading out of town.
  4. Stay on Klipheuwel Road and follow the map for the final turn to Goedgeleven.
FROM CAPE TOWN INTERNATIONAL AIRPORT
  1. Follow signs for the R300 heading north, towards Bellville / Brackenfell.
  2. Take the R300 until it joins the N1, then take the N1 towards Cape Town.
  3. Take Exit 23 (Okavango Road / R302) towards Durbanville.
  4. Continue onto Klipheuwel Road and follow the map for the final turn to Goedgeleven.

Please note: travel times on the Friday will be affected by traffic. Allow at least an hour to drive to the venue — or more if you’re travelling from the Southern Suburbs.

STAY OVER
Accommodation
◆

The wedding venue is about 40 minutes outside Cape Town (over an hour in traffic) and the Thursday events are in the Southern Suburbs. If you’re coming from out of town, your main options are probably:

  1. Stay near us in the Southern Suburbs (Wynberg, Constantia, Tokai, Kenilworth) — near the Thursday events, and drive through for the wedding.
  2. Stay near the wedding venue — limited accommodation at the venue itself, a few guest houses nearby, or anywhere in Durbanville.
  3. Stay somewhere in the middle — Cape Town CBD, Rondebosch or Century City.
Near the wedding venue

A few options near the venue in Durbanville. We’ll add more if we find them — but we have no control over actual availability.

Attire
◆

For the wedding we encourage traditional Indian attire in bright, festive colours. You don’t need to be Indian to join in — we’d love to see everyone dressed for the occasion.

Please avoid pure white (worn for funerals), red & gold (the bride’s colours), and jeans. The Thursday events are more relaxed.
